Information of Group Organization and Team Composition

  • Dr. Santiago Cerviño López: (group coordinator) Expert in fisheries biology and assessment methods, member of the Advisory Committee (ACOM) of ICES and the WGBIE and WGSAM Working Groups. He has participated in numerous European projects (VI and VII PM) as coordinator of Case Studies (BECAUSE, UNCOVER, ECOKNOWS) and WPs (MAREFRAME).

  • Dra. Begoña Santos Vázquez (former): Expert in many areas of marine biology and ecology and fisheries research, including small pelagic fisheries in the Northeast Atlantic (in particular the relationship between abundance and distribution of major fish species and the environment) and the management of the Iberian sardine stock. During 2014-2017 she was the Head of the Fisheries area at the IEO, and my work included the coordination and management of the IEO’s research and advisory activity in the area of fisheries to provide support to the national and regional administrations in relation to the monitoring, assessment, management and conservation of the natural resources. Participation in 28 international projects, 103 papers published in peer-reviewed journals, as well as 3 books and 11 book chapters.

  • Cristina Rodríguez-Cabello: is a senior researcher at the Spanish Institute of oceanography (IEO) responsible for the study of the biology and ecology of deep-sea elasmobranchs. She has participated in various research projects related to the biology of fish, the study of the communities of the bottom of the Cantabrian Sea shelf (ECOMARG, INDEMARES). Currently, she also participates in the projects EVALICES and IMPRESS aimed at analyzing and improving the diagnostic models on the state of exploitation of the different living resources that are of interest to the Spanish fleets operating in the ICES area. She has published about 30 articles in evaluated scientific journals. She has presented around 50 contributions at national and international congress and to several international working groups (ICCAT, ICES, OSPAR, EEA). She is a member of the elasmobranch working group (WGEF) at the International Commission for the Exploration of the Sea (ICES).

  • Esther Abad Casas: Experience in fisheries research. 32 research projects, 18 publications, 56 communications.

  • Dra. Paz Sampedro Pastor: Expert in identification and assessment of demersal stocks and biology and ecology of commercial shellfish. 16 research projects, 14 publications.

  • Dra. Marta Cousido Rocha: Mathematician, with a PhD in Statistics. Currently working at the C.O. Vigo, "Instituto Español de Oceanografía" at the Fishery Department acquiring new knowledge in the field Managament Strategy Evaluation (MSE).

  • Francisco Izquierdo Tarín: PhD student working on stock assessment and spatio-temporal distribution modeling at the "Instituto Español de Oceanografía" IEO (C.O. de Vigo) in the framework of IMPRESS project. Student member of the Statistical Modeling Ecology group (SMEG) from the University of Valencia and research assistant in the PREFERENTIAL project from the University of Minho. Member of the ICES workshop WKTaDSA.

  • Dra. Maria Isabel Riveiro: Expert in ichthyoplankton ecology, responsible of ichthyoplankton surveys CAREVA and JUREVA in the Iberian Peninsula, member of the ICES WGHANSA, WGACEGG and WGMEGS Working Groups.

  • Dra. Juan Gil: More than 20 years of experience in fisheries biology, including stock assessment at ICES (WGDEEP) and GFCM (WGSAD) working groups. Participation in different European and Spanish projects.

  • Dra. Margarita María Rincón Hidalgo: is a head scientist at IEO. She has a background on mathematics from the Colombian National University (Universidad Nacional de Colombia) and her PhD was about mathematical applications to Fisheries Science. Since then, her research is mainly focused on stock assessment models and the incorporation of environmental drivers on them. She has long experience participating in national and international research and innovation projects (ECOKNOWS, MareFrame, FarFish) and she is an official national member of the ICES groups for assessment WGHANSA and WGMIXFISH (Working Group on Southern Horse Mackerel, Anchovy, and Sardine and Working Group on Mixed Fisheries Advice).

  • Dra. Isabel González Herraiz: Expert in Norway lobster fisheries and fishing data analysis. Participation in 35 projects, 20 publications, 26 communications.

  • Dra. María Isabel Riveiro Alarcón: Expert in ichthylankton ecology and evaluation of sardine. Participation in 31 research projects, 29 publications.

  • Dr. Pablo Carrera López: Expert in sardine fishery, fisheries assessment and in the study of dynamics pelagic populations with hydroacoustic techniques.

  • Dra. María Rosario Domínguez Petit: Expert in reproductive ecology and analysis of life history of exploited marine species.

  • Valentín Trujillo Gorbea: He has been Principal Researcher of numerous IEO research projects, namely: Demersal ICES, Project of Fishing Databases, Monitoring and Analysis of Fishing Activity and finally, Principal Researcher of the IEO System Modeling and Analysis a cross-sectional project. He actively participated in international projects and different Methodological and Demersal Fisheries Resources Assessment Working Groups of the ICES. ICES International Coordinator for the Assessment of the Southern Stock of Hake (1992-1993). ICES International Coordinator for the Assessment of the Northern Stock of Hake (1994-2002). President of the ICES Hake, Monkfish, Megrim and Norway lobster Stock Assessment Working Group (2003-2005). Since 1994, independent expert of the EU (Subgroups of the Scientific, Technical and Economic Committee for Fisheries (STECF). Adviser to the EU, representing ICES in the area of ​​fisheries, for the beginning of the development of the EU Marine Strategy. Spanish Representative of the Advisory Committee for Fisheries Management (ACFM) of ICES (1998 - 2000, 2003-2007) and of the Resource Management Committee (RMC) of ICES (2005-2007). Director of the Vigo Oceanographic Center from October 2008 to June 2015. Carrying out numerous research works: scientific publications, book articles, scientific-technical working documents, advisory reports, conferences, etc. He has also participated in 22 oceanographic research surveys.

  • Dra. María Dolores Garabana Barro: Expert in reproductive ecology of exploited marine species ; Member and collaborator of the ICES groups ‘WGMEGS’ and ‘WGBIOP’.

  • María del Carmen Sainza Sousa : Biology expert (growth, maturity, length-to-weight relation, sex ratio, etc) of demersal stocks (hake, conger eel, mullet, etc).

  • Julio Valeiras Mota who has participated in national and international projects on marine resources in the Atlantic, Indian, Pacific and Mediterranean Sea.

  • María Grazia Pennino: Expert in marine biology, with a PhD in Mathematics and Statistics. Currently working at the C.O. Vigo, "Instituto Español de Oceanografía" at the Fishery Department.
